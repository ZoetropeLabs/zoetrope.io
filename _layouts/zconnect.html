We create hardware that will interface with your product’s existing electronics to collect useful data.

This hardware is tailored around what’s already in the product; we can add new sensors to collect data or use existing sensors and just add the required hardware to connect the product to the internet via 3G, wi-fi or LPWA.

The hardware can mount onto existing products, which is especially cost-effective and great for prototyping. We can also work with your product designers to seamlessly integrate the required hardware into your product.

Throughout our consultative process, we design, improve and refine the hardware to ensure that the final product will be built to provide the maximum business value for you.

Our cloud-based data analytics help you save money and get more from your product from day one.

We create a bank of data that belongs to your organisation and is securely stored in the cloud. This gives you the flexibility to explore different ways of using the data within your business, without having to have an in-house tech team.

We can customise our platform to include predictive analytics so that it will predict product faults and identify usage patterns. Access to the data can be controlled on a user basis to ensure it remains secure.

{% include image-mask-left.html path="/assets/img/whatwedo/code.jpg" %}

We have iOS, Android and web base apps for our zConnect platform, which we will customise to give you easy access to actionable insights.

Our custom dashboards digest the data so you are presented with the insight required for you to be able to make decisions. You can receive push notifications that are triggered by set events or by the predictive analytics. You are also able to control your product through the dashboard, so it’s easy to act on these notifications.

If you want more information, you can drill down into specific events, filter the data and find historical data.

We can create a number role based access groups to tailor access to your data around your business needs. We create custom dashboards for each access-level so that people see the insights that are relevant and valuable to them. Creating new accounts and user-levels is easy so the product can scale with your business
