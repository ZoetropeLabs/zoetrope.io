Consult

1 week

We work with you to create a plan for connecting your products that will maximise the gains and minimise any risks or costs. We enjoy working with both technical directors and business directors; whatever your level of technical expertise, we are confident communicating the right information for you to be able to make sound business decisions.

{% include image-mask-right.html path="/assets/img/approach/consult.jpg" %}

Design & Prototype

6-8 weeks

We have a modular system ready to integrate with your product, we use elements of this where appropriate to speed up the delivery and reduce your costs and project risk. We are adept at designing custom modules, so we can meet your unique business needs and integrate with your existing systems.

We will build a prototype of your product in our lab, which allows you to trial the system before signing off a final design or incurring any production manufacturing costs.

{% include image-mask-left.html path="/assets/img/approach/design.jpg" %}

Trial & Refine

6 week trial, 8 week refinement

We’re always looking for ways to make the product work harder for you, whether that be through advanced analytics or adding new connected modules. This phase allows us to gather key insights and work with you to maximise the value of the product for your business.

At this stage we’ll help you integrate connectivity into your products in a production-ready capacity.

{% include image-mask-right.html path="/assets/img/approach/refine.jpg" %}

Go live

Circa 16 week process overall

You and your customers start benefiting from the new connectivity of your product and the actionable insight from day one. Licensing of the product includes email and phone support from us, to give you peace of mind and to ensure that everything is working correctly.

We provide consultation for continually developing your product alongside your business. If you want to take maintenance, support and development in house, we will work with you to ensure a smooth transition.
